Test Your Internet Speed with the OpenSpeedTest

Slow or unreliable Internet? Take a speed test to measure the quality of your household’s Internet service.

What is a speed test?  A speed test measures the speed and quality of your Internet service at a point in time.

Why should I take a speed test?  Taking a speed test may help explain your Internet experience, by identifying the limits of – and any problems with – your Internet service.

Speed Test Best Practices

  • Take your speed test close to a router.
  • Plug into a router using an ethernet cord, if you have one.
  • You may take your speed test during peak usage time – (generally in the evening) – but, avoid those times when others in your household may be streaming videos or gaming.
